About Me

I'm Humberto Godinez, currently I'm a Specialist Data Scientist in the Business Development area of QRI. Previously, I served as a Quantitative Advisor to the CFO and the Risk Managing Director of Pemex. During my 10 year career at Pemex, I was responsible for developing models to forecast energy prices, tariffs, the P&L statement, and the evaluation of strategic projects. I also led a division for procurement optimization of one of the business lines of the Company. As a Specialist with QRI, I'm currently working on big data analytics and machine learning projects, as well as economic modelling and risk evaluation.

I hold a PhD in Mathematics from The Institute for Financial and Actuarial Mathematics of The University of Liverpool. I also studied Actuarial Sciences and Applied Mathematics, a Master in Public Policy and I have several Certifications inlcuding, Financial Management, Econometric Modelling and Data Sciences. My research agenda is focused on the important subject of designing systems that are financially sustainable during a period of ageing risks. I belive my work and the emerging results have considerable practical and policy-making significance at national and international levels.
